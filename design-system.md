**Канонические значения цветов, отступов и motion — в `:root` в `styles.css`.** Ниже — краткое арт-направление.

Art direction
Tone: modern, confident, premium, clean.
Mood: AI-native, but not cliché.
Visual principle: neutral surfaces + one restrained accent.
Avoid: фиолетовые градиенты, glowing blobs, кислотные неоны, “3 одинаковые feature cards с иконками в кружках”.

Цвета
Background: #0B1020
Surface: #11182B
Surface-2: #151F36
Border: rgba(255,255,255,0.08)
Text primary: #F3F7FF
Text muted: #A6B3CC
Accent: #5EEAD4
Accent hover: #2DD4BF

Сетка и отступы
Container: 1200–1280px
Section padding desktop: 96–120px
Section padding mobile: 56–72px
Grid: 12 columns desktop, 1 column mobile
Radius: 20–28px on large cards, 14–16px on controls
Use only spacing tokens from a 4px scale.

Компоненты
Primary button: solid accent, dark text, slight lift on hover.
Secondary button: transparent, 1px subtle border.
Cards: darker surface, soft border, small shadow, no bright gradients.
Badges: tiny uppercase or compact pill, muted background with accent text.
Numbers/stats: big numerals, quiet labels.
